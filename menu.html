ABOUT US

BELIEFS

A state of the art gym for fitness lovers with a passion for variety.
After noticing we couldn't find a studio that offered enough to consistently motivate us, we decided to create one! MyFitness Suites is a fitness facility that provides a mix of instructor-led classes for improved strength, endurance, flexibility, and wellness.
Mix and match weight training, boot camp, hot yoga, HIIT, hot barre, pilates, functional fitness, and more.
Our classes cost less than a babysitter and we offer that to our members for free!
Our vision is to create a community of like-minded people who share in the mission of health and wellness for all. We owe it to ourselves and our families to be healthy in mind, body, and spirit.
MyFitness Suites is run by a group of trainers who own and share in this vision.
They are leaders in their communities and the best at their craft.
Come join us for a class and join in the movement.


MISSION & VISION

Engage by inspiring the fitness community through authentic relationships, social media and competition presence
Equip our community by providing enriching content, “True Strength” weekend training camps, chapter events in cities around the world, and an “IMPACT” conference for chapter leadership teams.
Expand when our leaders and those whom they influence are becoming mature disciples of Christ, making new disciples, and serving their communities.
